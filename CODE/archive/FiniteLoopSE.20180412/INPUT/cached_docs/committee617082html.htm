ISO/TC 254

Safety of amusement rides and amusement devices

 

About

  • Secretariat: GOST R
    Secretary:
    Ms Anna Novikova
  • Chairperson (until end 2019):
    Dr Vladimir Gnezdilov
  • ISO Technical Programme Manager :
    Mme Blandine Garcia
    ISO Editorial Programme Manager :
    Mr Bastien Gavoille
  • Creation date: 2010

Scope

Standardization in the field of safety of amusement rides and amusement devices.

Excluded:

  • playgrounds, facilities and equipment covered by International Standards within the scope of ISO/TC 83.
