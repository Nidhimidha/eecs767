ISO/TC 207

Environmental management

 

About

  • Secretariat: SCC
    Secretary:
    Ms Vanessa Mitchell
  • Chairperson (until end 2020):
    Ms Sheila Leggett
    Vice chairperson (until end 2021):
    Mr Daniel Trillos
  • ISO Technical Programme Manager :
    Mr José Alcorta
    ISO Editorial Programme Manager :
    Ms Nicola Perou
  • Creation date: 1993

Scope

Standardization in the field of environmental management systems and tools in support of sustainable development.

Excluded:

  • test methods of pollutants, setting limit values and levels of environmental performance, and standardization of products.

Note:

The TC for environmental management will have close cooperation with ISO / TC 176 in the field of environmental systems and audits.
